About the App

Welcome to the Tree Planting Spacing Calculator! This tool helps you determine the appropriate spacing for planting various types of trees, as well as the optimal number of seedlings on your land, based on the dimensions of your plot and the selected planting pattern. Whether you want to maximize space usage or follow specific planting guidelines, our app provides useful insights and recommendations to support healthy growth and efficient land use. Stay tuned for updates and additional features!
